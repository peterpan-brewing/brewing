## Instructions

1. Steep grains (Malts and oat flakes) (30L of water, 65~70°C 90min)
2. Add rice hulls and raise the temperature to 78°C (Took 13min)
3. Lauter until clear (The first wort came out to 15.9L.)
4. Sparge (Add 18L of 80°C water)
5. Boil the wort for 120 minutes, extracting additional wort from the other side
6. Add hops after 30 minutes of boiling.
7. Add molasses and maple syrup after 110 minutes of boiling.
8. 4.8L of sparge water was added during the boil.
9. After boiling, the final volume was 22.6L.
10. Filter out hop residue and clove through a sieve
11. Aerate the wort
12. Add yeast
13. Ferment for 10 days
14. Put the spices back into the mesh bags, place them in the fermentation tank, and then ferment for an additional 10 days
15. Bottle with white sugar (1.88g per 330ml)
